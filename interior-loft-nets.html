Custom Mezzanine Solutions

The custom-made interior net, also called a housing net or hammock floor, is a net stretched into a frame like a mezzanine to fill a void in your house, apartment, or cottage.

Instead of an empty space, Damannets offers a unique relaxation area where you can relax, read, or enjoy a nap. Our nets allow light to pass through, maintaining the brightness of the lower levels while adding a stylish and functional living space.

Installation & Technical Requirements

A solid framework is essential for installing a mezzanine net. This can be made of wood, IPN (steel beams), or concrete.

Spacing: Fasteners (eye bolts or brackets) should be placed every 150mm around the frame for optimal tension and safety.

Lacing: A high-tensile tension rope (available in 6mm, 8mm, or 10mm) is laced between the net and the fasteners to pull the hammock floor tight.
