Interior Hammock Floor & Loft Nets

Custom-made interior nets to fill voids in your home, creating a safe, friendly, and atypical relaxation space.

Transform Your Empty Spaces

The custom-made interior net, also known as a housing net or hammock floor, is a net stretched into a frame like a mezzanine to fill a void in your house, apartment, or cottage.

Damannets provides a unique solution to turn unused vertical space into a comfortable lounging area. Our nets are fixed tightly to offer a flat surface that allows light to pass through while providing superior comfort for adults and children alike.
