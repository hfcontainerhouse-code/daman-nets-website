Technical Excellence & Comfort

We use knotless nets for maximum guest comfort. Our edges are meticulously crafted with 8-10mm high-tensile polyester ropes to ensure perfect tension and security.

All materials are UV-treated and salt-water resistant, specifically designed for the harsh reverberation of lagoons and coastal environments.
